This album was created as a love letter to several of my favorite electronic genres.
Each song draws inspiration from a different artist or genre.
It is the first project to fully feature my vocals.
The lyrics are a tender retrospective of the revival of my faith in Jesus Christ after an extended period of unbelief, and the album is named thusly.
